About Campus Cafe

Delicious food made with students in mind

Our Story

Campus Cafe was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: provide students and faculty with quality food at affordable prices. We understand the challenges of student life - tight budgets, busy schedules, and the need for nutrition that powers academic success.

What started as a small coffee cart has grown into the cafe you see today. We're proud to be part of the campus community and committed to growing with it.
